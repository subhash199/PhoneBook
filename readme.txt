PhoneBook

The phonebook will show the previously saved contacts in the database once lauched.

Clicking on Add Contact - will launch a new window, fill in the details to save contact. The details
are sensitive as in no number or special charactes in the names field and no letter or special characters
in phone field. The phone number needs to 11 number in length to be valid.

You can Click on Edit button to edit the details or delete the contact from the Database.

You can use the search bar to search for any name or phone number.
